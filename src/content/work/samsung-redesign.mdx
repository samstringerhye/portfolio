# Samsung.com Redesign

Samsung.com serves hundreds of millions of visitors across dozens of markets. The design system had accumulated debt, and the global navigation wasn't holding up. This engagement focused on building consistency at scale: a tightened design system and a nav architecture that could work everywhere Samsung sells.

## Approach

As a Senior Designer on a large cross-functional team, I evolved and applied the design system across key site areas, designed page layouts to Samsung's brand standards, and proposed and iterated on GNB updates. The nav had to work across a dozen-plus markets, so there were a lot of rounds.
